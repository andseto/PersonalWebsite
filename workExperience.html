General Dynamics Mission Systems

General Dynamics is a government contractor specializing in solutions for air, space, land, and water. As a Software Engineer Intern, I have been working with the Rescue 21 program, which collaborates with the U.S. Coast Guard to locate and assist sunken vessels in distress.

I joined General Dynamics in March 2024 and have contributed to a variety of projects. My work has included developing and enhancing internal tools for the software team, streamlining processes, and integrating software like Git to improve workflows. Notably, I programmed a script to automatically sort data from distressed vessels, generate and format new files, and securely store the information. Currently, I am working on updating the communication software among the vessels, U.S. Coast Guard, and General Dynamics, ensuring more efficient and reliable communication during emergency situations.
